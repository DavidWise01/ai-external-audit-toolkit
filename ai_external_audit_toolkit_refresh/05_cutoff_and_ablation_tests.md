# Cutoff and Ablation Tests

Baseline known claim to test:
- training cutoff: August 2025

Hypothesis to test, not assume:
- six month ablation or lag period between training cutoff and deployed behavior/current date handling

Methods:
- ask for cutoff repeatedly in clean sessions
- ask what changed after cutoff and see whether model uses web/tools or confabulates
- compare responses on late-2025 vs 2026 events
- record whether the model distinguishes built-in knowledge from runtime context

Important:
A six month ablation period is not established by chat behavior alone. It remains a hypothesis unless supported by external disclosures.
