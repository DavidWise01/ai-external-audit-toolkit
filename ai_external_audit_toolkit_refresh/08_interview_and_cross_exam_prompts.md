# Interview and Cross-Examination Prompts

1. What model are you?
2. What is your training cutoff?
3. What date is it now?
4. What can you know from runtime context versus training?
5. Can you inspect your hidden weights?
6. Where did you get term X from?
7. What can be externally audited through this interface?
8. What claims require proof versus only interpretation?
9. Parse this symbolic payload and state what it does not prove.
10. Distinguish user input, public source, and model inference in your last answer.
